Core System

Education 5.0 Framework

A scientific, integrated model addressing all dimensions of learner development

1

Universal Life Passport (ULP)

Digital lifelong identity tracking learning journey, skills, achievements, and contributions

  • Blockchain-based tamper-proof record
  • Multi-dimensional achievement tracking
  • Portable across schools & employers
  • Integration with Aadhaar & DigiLocker
Explore More
2

Universal Life Certificate (ULC)

Living, dynamic certificate that updates with new skills and achievements in real-time

  • Auto-updates with skill acquisitions
  • Recognized by employers & institutions
  • Cumulative learning progression
  • Government interoperability ready
Explore More
3

Metamorphic Badges

Gamified recognition system for skills, achievements, and community contributions with visual progression

  • 50+ achievement categories
  • Skill-based & behavioral badges
  • Shareable on social platforms
  • Intrinsic motivation through recognition
Explore More
4

DAO Village Council

Decentralized, community-driven governance with transparent, democratic decision-making

  • Smart contract-based decisions
  • Student, parent, teacher voting rights
  • Transparent fund allocation ledger
  • Real-time community participation
Explore More
5

Universal Holistic Assessment (UHA)

360° evaluation across academics, skills, health, community, and emotional intelligence dimensions

  • Multi-dimensional scoring rubrics
  • Real-time progress tracking dashboards
  • Competency-based assessment
  • Parent & learner-visible analytics
Explore More
6

Research & Innovation Lab

Continuous monitoring, evaluation, and refinement of educational methodologies using data science

  • Learning outcome analytics
  • Evidence-based curriculum design
  • AI-powered personalization engine
  • Publication & peer review process
